How I Spent My Thanksgiving

Third Grade Paper

Written by Michael Sprague (November 26, 1984)
On Thursday morning I woke up and started making cards for my reletives. At 2:00 we got ready for the trip to Owego when we got there it was 3:30. My uncle and I looked at some of his coledge news papers. Later we went to my uncle's house that we were going to eat at. At 6:27 we ate. When we were done I was so full. My Grandpa, Uncle Mike, Uncle Pete and I took a walk. When we got home my Aunt Jody was drunk. My mom finally got her to go to sleep. I didn't get to sleep until 2:05. (sic)
